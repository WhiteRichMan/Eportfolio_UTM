Assignment 4 - MaGICX Visit

Reflection: For Assignment 4, we are required to conduct an industrial visit to MaGICX. MaGICX is a research that focuses on VR and AR application in daily life. This industrial visit excited me as I was very interested in VR and AR technology and from this visit, I am able to have hands on experience with the VR technology showcase, which are VRLab and VRSports. From this visit, I have learnt that in IT industry, there would be lots developing and researching process of new technology where the new technology will be able to help others in solving problems. Other than that, I am very excited to learn more on technologies that are going to emerge among IT industry. For improvement, I would suggest myself to have better communication with my group members when doing our assignment so that misunderstanding may be avoid and better ideas may be able to be conveyed during the assignment.
